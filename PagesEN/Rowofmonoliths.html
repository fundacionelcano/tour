Row of Monoliths

In addition to the cemetery, the necropolis had a ceremonial area. It contains three types of archaeological stone structures. This includes an alignment of 67 columnar basalt formations 105 meters in length, and another 14 similar columns 67 meters in length. Next to them were a group of 26 stone figures, 2 altars, 2 basaltic columns with carved reliefs, and 37 sculptures in tuff and basalt.
