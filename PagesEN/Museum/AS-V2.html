1-2. Anthropomorphic pitchers.
They represent the officiants of the funerary ritual in charge of taking the offerings to the tombs. They wear red capes, and carry a lump on their backs and a jug on their heads. Like the warriors, they also wore painted arms and faces.

3. Carafe. Next to the bodies and on the mortuary chambers the officiants of the ritual placed, as offering, thousands of ceramics of different crockery: polychrome, smoked, red, with decoration in panels.
