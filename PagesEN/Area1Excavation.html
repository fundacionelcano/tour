Area 1 Excavation

**This area is only available for viewing when the archaeological team is working**

In this area Dr. Julia Mayo and the El Caño Foundation team have been excavating since 2008. This area is identified as Area 1, and dates between 680 A.D. - 1000 A.D. Within this area there are 7 tombs containing multiple burials.

The team can only excavate during the dry season, which is December through April. Excavations are normally two months, depending on funding. Artifacts recovered from these tombs are on display at the museum.
